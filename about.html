Hello! Welcome to my personal site!

My name is Jixiong LIU, and I’m currently a Ph.D. student of Sorbonne University at the institution of research EURECOM supervised by Raphaël Troncy, I also work as a data scientist researcher in the DATA-IA entity at Orange Labs since 2020 directed by Yoan Chabot.
My research topic focuses on Knowledge Engineering and Natural Language Processing, more specifically, I’m interested in:

  • Semantic interpretation of tabular data
  • Table process and data augmentation
  • Knowledge representation

Chinese is my native language, but I’m fluent in English (for academics) and French (for work & life), also I could understand some basic Japanese.

During my free time, I love writing songs with my guitar and my piano, also mangas. And I’m also an RPG Gamer.

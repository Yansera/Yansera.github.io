Hello! Welcome to my personal site!

I’m currently a PhD student for the institution of research EURECOM supervised by Raphaël Troncy， I also work as a Data Scientist in the DATA-IA entity at Orange Labs since 2020 supervised by Yoan Chabot.
My research topic focuses on knowledge engineering and Natural language processing, more specifically, I’m interested in:

  • Semantic Interpretation of Tabular data
  • Graph modeling
  • Natural language processing

During the free time. I love writing songs with my guitar and my piano. And I’m an RPG Gamer.
